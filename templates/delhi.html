Here in this website, we have analysed various aspects of Delhi's housing from the data which we have collected so that the users can take informed decisions before searching for houses

Area

This image shows the most spacious and least spacious localities to live in Delhi. Spaciousness of a locality is calculated by taking the average of areas of houses in that locality. Most spacious localities are the localities that have the highest average area of all the houses in that locality and converse is true for the least spacious localities. From this image, we see that Bhawani Kunj,Karawal Nagar, South Extension II are the least spacous localities in Delhi whereas Geetanjali Enclave,South Extension Part 1 and Niti Bagh are the most spacious localities in Delhi

Affordability

This image shows the most afforable and least afforable localities to live in Delhi. Affordability of a locality is calculated by taking the average price of all the houses in that locality. Most affordable localities are the localities that have the lowest average price and the converse is true for least afforable localities. From this image, we see that Tilangpur Kotla, Karawal Nagar and Ghevra are the most afforable localities in Delhi whereas Sarojini Nagar,Diplomatic Enclave and Sundar Nagar are the least affordable localities in Delhi

Other

Here we have analyzed other aspects of the houses in Delhi which are

  1. Seller Type - The entity which is putting the property on rent - Can be of 3 types
    • Agent - The real estate agent through which the owner would like to put the property on rent
    • Builder - The builder of the property who might put the property on rent
    • Owner - The owner may directly put the property on rent
  2. Layout Type - The layout of the property put on rent
    • RK - The property contains only a Room and Kitchen where the bedroom and hall are combined
    • BHK - The property contains a Bedroom, Hall and a Kitchen
  3. Property Type - The type of property which is put on rent
    • Apartment - A flat in a building which contains many flats
    • Studio Aparment - Contains only one room
    • Independent House - A single house with boundary walls on four sides
    • Independent Floor - An entire floor on a building isolated from the rest of the building
    • Villa
    • Penthouse
  4. Furnish Type
    • Semi Furnished - Contains some furniture sufficient enough for the tennant to settle
    • Furnished - Contains all the necessary furniture for a tennant to settle
    • Unfurnished - Contains no furnitire
